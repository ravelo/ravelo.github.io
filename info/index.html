About me

I’m drawn to work that makes an impact. And I believe that, with the right mix of facilitation skill and systems thinking, designers can have an important role to play in addressing the social, technical, and economic components of problems worth solving.

With a background in journalism, I tend to ask a lot of questions in order to make sure we’re working on the right problem, framing that problem correctly, and moving at the most effective speed. I’m also currently learning how to be a great design mentor. ✨
